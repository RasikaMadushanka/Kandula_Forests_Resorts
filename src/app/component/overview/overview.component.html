Kandula Forests Resort is a serene nature retreat located near the Udawalawe forest, surrounded by lush greenery and a calm, silent environment. It is the perfect place for guests who wish to relax and enjoy the beauty of nature.

We offer friendly service, comfortable stays, and guided wildlife experiences to help guests see animals and explore the area. With personalized care and a variety of services, we ensure every guest enjoys a peaceful and memorable stay
